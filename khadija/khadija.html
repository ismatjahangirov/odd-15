CHESS COMPETITION

There are some international competitions in the world.Chess is one of the important and interesting games.It is particularly connected with the brain.Chess is one of the oldest intellectual games that combines sports, scientific thinking, and elements of art.
Two sides play chess:black and white. The goal of the game is to checkmate the opponent's king, which means creating a situation where the king has no defense against attacking pieces and will be captured (removed from the board) in the next move.The game takes place on a square board with 64 equal squares, eight along each side. The horizontal rows are numbered from 1 to 8, and the vertical rows are labeled with Latin letters from a to h.

The names of chess figures:
  • King
  • Queen
  • Rook
  • Bishop
  • Knight
  • Pawn
